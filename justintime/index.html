    Who we are?

    To cater for the needs of catering houses food business and individuals that would like to have their animals slaughtered under hygienic conditions. Our company is in partnership with the association of national poultry farmers and regional farmers association such as large commodity traders operating in large regional produce market such as Salaga, Kintampo and Techiman and to bring quality products to our customers.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to become the leading meat processing plant in West Africa and to create the required hygienic environment for the slaughtering and preparation of animals for the domestic market.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide an international cold store services and distribution of agricultural commodities both locally and within the West Africa trade hub.
